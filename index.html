About Us

The Glass House Initiative is a non-profit journal dedicated to providing affordable housing resources and support to the community of Marquette, MI. Our mission is to create a transparent and supportive environment for those seeking housing solutions.

Resources

We offer a variety of resources including listings of affordable housing options, guides on how to apply for housing assistance, and connections to local organizations that provide support.

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference! Whether you want to volunteer, donate, or simply spread the word, there are many ways to get involved with the Glass House Initiative.

Contact

If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact us via email at info@glasshouseinitiative.org or call us at (123) 456-7890.
